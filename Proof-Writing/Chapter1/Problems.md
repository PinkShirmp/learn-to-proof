1.
A x B
Given $A=\{1,2,3,4\}$ and $B=\{a,c\}$
$$
\begin{aligned}
A*B = \{(1,a),(1,c),(2,a),(2,c),(3,a),(3,c),(4,a),(4,c)\}
\\
A*A=A^2=\{(x,y):x,y \in A\}
\\
\emptyset * B= \emptyset
\\
(B*B)=\{(a,a),(a,c),(c,c)\}
\\
A*(B*B)= A* \{(a,a),(c,c),(a,c)\} = \\ \{(1,(a,a)),(1,(c,c)),(1,(c,c)),(2,(a,a)),...,(4,(c,c))\}



\end{aligned}
$$
